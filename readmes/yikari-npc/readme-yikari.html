Introduction

Yikari is monk NPC for BGII. In Shadows of Amn, he can be found in the Temple District in Athkatla (on the bridge generally in the middle of the area) and may be be summoned by the Throne of Bhaal Fate Spirit.

Yikari is Lawful Neutral and has the following statistics:

  • STR 16
  • INT 8
  • WIS 18
  • DEX 18
  • CON 14
  • CHR 4

This mod provides good characters a roleplaying reason to get Celestial Fury. Yikari's quest is to retrieve the sword, and return it to its rightful owner and location. Whether or not the player already has the sword at their first meeting affects Yikari's equipment, biography, and dialogue, for Yikari has taken a vow of silence until he found has the sword.

Once the party gets Celestial Fury, he can be persuaded to stay in your party. Under normal circumstances, he will keep the sword for himself, and not allow the PC to use it. Charismatic PCs can persuade Yikari that another character should wield Celestial Fury (Yikari must be in the party, however).

Installation

Windows

Yikari is packaged and installed with WeiDU and is distributed as a NSIS-installer. To install, simply double-click the Yikari-v1.7.exe and follow the instructions on screen.
